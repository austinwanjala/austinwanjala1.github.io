How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost for the dental equipment or products you need, give us a quick phone call. We'll ask you a few questions about your dental practice's requirements, the type of products you're interested in, and any additional services you may need. From there, we'll provide you with a rough estimate. If you're satisfied, we'll schedule a visit to your practice to offer a detailed quote tailored to your needs.

How Long Will It Take to Deliver Your Dental Products?

he delivery time for dental products depends on your specific order and requirements. If you have a time frame in mind, we will do our best to meet it. A common delay in fulfilling orders can occur if additional product information or special requests are required. Rest assured, we’ll keep you informed throughout the process to ensure timely delivery.

How Do I Partner with Your Dental Practice?

The process begins when you contact us with your dental product or equipment requirements. We carefully review your needs and discuss the best options available for your practice. Based on our conversation, you can choose a solution that best fits your dental practice. After that, we begin the process of supplying and delivering the products, ensuring everything is tailored to your specific needs.

+

When Do I Pay for My Dental Products?

For most orders, we offer flexible payment options, with payments typically made at the start, midway, and upon delivery. However, we are happy to work with you to set up a payment schedule that suits your needs. We understand that purchasing dental products is a significant investment, and we want to make the process as manageable as possible for you. We accept payments via credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer.
